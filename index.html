Carbon Tiptoe

Carbon Tiptoe is an online tool designed to help users calculate and understand their current carbon footprint as well as compare their results to other users. With the current threat of global warming, there is an ever larger emphasis on being aware and making self conscious decisions on the factors that contribute to our C02 emissions. Carbon Tiptoe offers an interactive survey for the user to complete, following the complete the user will have tailored results presented to them on useful changes that they can implement to improve their C02 emissions.

Why Carbon Tiptoe?

As well as supplying tailored and specific results, Carbon Tiptoe offers users the ability to compare their carbon footprint to others. This involves global users as well as those in the local area. This further allows the user to understand their impact on those around them as well as allowing the user to create local movements if they see fit.
